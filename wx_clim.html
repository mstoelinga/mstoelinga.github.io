Note, with Individual radars, can customize url:

  • https://a.atmos.washington.edu/~ovens/loops/wxloop.cgi?radar_atx_ref1+/2h
  • Replace atx with rtx (Portland, OR), lgx (Langley Hill, near Ocean Shore, WA), or otx (Spokane, WA).
  • Replace ref (reflectivity) with vel (Doppler velocity).
  • Replace 1 (lowest elevation angle) with 2, 3, 4 (higher elevation angles).

  • You’ll know it’s the regular (non-extended) menu page because the background is NOT pink.

  • You’ll know it’s the extended menu page because the background is pink.
  • Runs after regular run is complete, goes out to forecast hour 240, and only has 36 and 12-km domains
